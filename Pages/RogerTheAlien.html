Roger The Alien
This VFX video I created explores the journey of a pro-life individual whose social media efforts aim to protect life, even beyond Earth. Inspired by American Dad, the story takes an unexpected turn as it's revealed that he isn't quite who he seems. With a blend of humor and intrigue, the video uses visual effects to amplify the twist, offering a fresh perspective on identity and the lengths one will go to in defense of life.
