Flash
This VFX video I created blends the electrifying speed of DC's "The Flash" with the unpredictable nature of Canadian timing and weather. By combining superhero action with the elements, I explored the idea of how speed and extreme weather interact in a visually compelling way. The result is a high-energy, dynamic piece that plays with contrasts, offering a fresh take on both The Flash's abilities and the challenges of Canada's ever-changing climate.
